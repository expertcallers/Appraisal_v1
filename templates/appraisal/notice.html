EVALUATION RATING SCALE
Rating Scale Indicator Description
4.5 to 5 Outstanding Demonstrates highest standards of performance excellence and achievement of individual goals. Significantly exceeds objectives
4.0 to 4.49 Excellent Exceeds most Position requirements & Expectations. Shows exceptional performance in some aspects of the position
3.5 to 3.9 Good Meets most position requirements and expectations. Shows strong promise for future growth and higher attainment
3 to 3.49 Satisfactory Demonstrates partial fulfillment of evaluation criteria in line with objectives
Below 3 Needs Improvement Does not meet position requirements and expectations. Overall performance does not match the quality and quantity of peers
